About Me

Kerby Diaz, better known as KERU, has quickly made a name for himself as a "dubstepper" from Los Angeles with a love for anime and EDM. Fusing his passions, KERU pushes the boundaries of the underground bass scene in the U.S., aiming to elevate it with infectious energy and meme culture. His goal is to spread the euphoria and joy that bass music consistently delivers.

Since beginning his grind in 2017, this South-Central LA-based producer has played shows across the country, selling out numerous stops. Notable performances include direct support for Swando in Utah and Jessica Audiffred at the House of Blues in San Diego. His music has also found its way to major festivals such as EDC, Lost Lands, Forbidden Kingdom, and Rampage. He has gained support from prominent artists like ATLiens, Riot Ten, Panda Eyes, Nasko, RayRay, Dirty Audio, and Death Code.

KERU's previous releases on labels such as Barong Family, Riddm Network, and Reinelex Records showcase his unique sound. With a vault full of unreleased tracks, fans can expect more major label releases soon. KERU aspires to make his mark not only in the dubstep scene but across the entire EDM community.
